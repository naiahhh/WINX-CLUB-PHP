CRMS is a web-based application that is designed to store, process, retrieve, and analyze information concerned with criminal records. It is a system used to record and store criminal records of individuals for future reference. The system helps law enforcement agencies to keep track of criminals and their criminal activities. It is designed to manage and store criminal records efficiently.
